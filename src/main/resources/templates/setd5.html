Phenotype of the month

setd5

SET domain containing 5

MICE at IMPC

Phenotype Summary(Heterozygots):

Recessive Lethality: No homozygous embros detecetd at E14.5 (n=32)
Heterozygous viability: Heterozygous x wild type breeding pairs generated wild type and heterozygous offspring at a ratio of 7:3 (n=425)
16 week PBL: increased percentage of Treg cells in females, increased percenage of monocytes in females
DSS colitis: Increased weight loss
Weight curve: Males have reduced weight
Dysmorpholgy: Abnormal hair colour and hair pattern in females, abnormal snout morphology in males and females, abnormal snout size in males
Micronuclei: Increased genomic instablility as detected by the increased presence of micronuclei

Gene information:

  • • Predicted to encode a methyltransferase
  • • Encoded on chromosome 3 in humans, in a region (3p25) associated with intellectual disability
  • • One of the genes often lost in 3p25 microdeletion syndrome
  • • Loss of function mutations lead to autosomal dominant mental retardation (MRD23)
