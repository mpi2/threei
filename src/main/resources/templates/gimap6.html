Phenotype of the month

Gimap6

GTPase, IMAP family member 6

Data at IMPC

Phenotype Summary:

6 week PBL: Decreased total T cell, αβ T cell and CD4 αβ T cell % females and males, Decreased NKT cell % females and males
16 week PBL: Increased monocyte % female and males
16 week Spleen: Decreased NKT cell % females and males
Salmonella Challenge: Abnormal weight loss

Gene information:

  • GTPases of the immunity associated protein family (Gimaps) are a family of 8 genes that encode putative small GTPases
  • Primarily expressed in cells of the immune system
  • Gimap family members implicated in T and B-cell development and survival
