Phenotype of the month

Wdtc1

WD and tetratricopeptide repeats 1 / adipose

Data at IMPC

Phenotype Summary:

Mesenteric lymph nodes: Increased CD8 T cells
Influenza Challenge: Increased weight loss

Gene information:

  • • Ortholog of the Drosophila gene adp (adipose)
  • • In humans, WDTC1 expresion in adipose tissue is associated with lower fat mass and enhanced insulin sensititvity
