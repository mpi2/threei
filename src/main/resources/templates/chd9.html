Phenotype of the month

Chd9

Chromodomain Helicase DNA Binding Protein 9

Data at IMPC

Phenotype Summary:

Epidermal Immune composition: Increased Langerhans cells in males and females
Plasma Chemistry: Decreased amylase in males and females, decreased albumin in males

Gene information:

  • Proposed role in chromatin remodelling
  • Involved in transcriptional regulation of bone tissue differentiation
