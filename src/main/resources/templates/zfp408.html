Phenotype of the month

Zfp408

zinc finger protein 408

MICE at IMPC

Phenotype Summary:

Ear epidermis: decreased count of Vγ5+ dendritic epidermal T cells (DETC) not contacting Langerhans cells
Eye morphology:no phenotype

Gene information:

  • • Predicted to be a DNA-binding molecule
  • • Crucial for the development of retinal vasculature in zebrafish
  • • Mutated in families with exudative vitreoretinopathy, a genetic disorder affecting the growth and development of blood vessels in the retina
  • • Mutated in families with Retinitis pigmentosa, a group of genetic disorders causing progressive visual impairment due to photo receptor cell death
  • • a CpG island at the 5' end and the wide tissue distribution may suggest a function as housekeeping gene
