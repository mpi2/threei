Phenotype of the month

Cxcr2

C-X-C chemokine receptor 2

Data at IMPC

Phenotype Summary:

16 week PBL - increased CD4 αβ effector T cells in males and females, increased CD8 αβ effector T cells in males, increased γδ T cells in males and females, decreased B cells in females, increased neutrophils in females and monocytes in females and males; no difference in heterozygous mice
Spleen - Increased effector CD4 and CD8 αβ T-cells, increased effector γδ T cells, increased granulocytes, increased eosinophils
Bone Marrow - Increased granulocytes
Plasma Chemistry - Decreased glucose and increased creatinine in heterozygous females; ecreased total protein in homozygous males and females
Anti-nuclear antibody assay - Increased anti-nuclear antibodies in serum
Salmonella Challenge - Increased weight loss and bacterial count in liver and ceacal contents

Gene information:

  • G-protein coupled receptor
  • involved in immune cell migration
  • expressed at high levels on circulating neutrophils
  • involved in senescence, cancer and diseases caused by activated neutrophils
