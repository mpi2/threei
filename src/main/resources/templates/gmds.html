Gmds

GDP mannose 4,6-dehydratase

Basic, immunoglobulin-like variable motif containing

Mice at IMPC

Phenotype Summary:

Ear epidermis: decreased count of Vγ5+ dendritic epidermal T cells (DETC), presence of CD45+ Vγ5- MHC II- replacement cells in the epidermis
Antinuclear antibodies: Increased level of antinuclear antibodies in serum
Mesenteric Lymph Node - Increased effector γδ cells and KLRG1+ CD8 αβ T cells
DSS colitis: Decreased suspectibility to induced colitis: reduced average histology score
Body composition (DEXA): Decreased bone mineral density in females

Gene information:

  • possesses no sequence similarity with any described protein, making the prediction of a function challenging
  • a CpG island at the 5' end and the wide tissue distribution may suggest a function as housekeeping gene
